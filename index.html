Manchester Munchies

Your guide to eating out in Manchester

About Us

We are a group of girls living in Manchester who are passionate about all things food!
We have created Manchester Munchies as a place to share the best places to eat out, based on our own experiences.
Manchester has a thriving, exciting – and growing – food scene on all levels, from cutting edge cocktails to top-notch steaks.
So whether you're a first time visitor or are already familiar with Manchester, have a browse and find something that suits you!

Asian

Asian cuisine can be found all over the city, ranging from Middle Eastern cusine to East Asian cusine. The 'curry mile' is a hotspot for a good curry and we also have our very only Chinatown, in which you can find delicious ramen, rice and noodle dishes!

Mexican

Mexican cuisine is a very popular theme in Manchester's restaurants and bars. From burritos bars to backstreet 'taquerías', we have got you covered.

English

Manchester is full of establishments which serve traditional English food. Definitely a must for anyone who is visiting and would like a taste of the English culture.

Street food

Street food is a new, thriving food scene in Manchester. It has created a more casual and social environment for dining, whilst still providing good quality food.
